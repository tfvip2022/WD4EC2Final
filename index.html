Nature in Hong Kong

Hong Kong is one of the financial centers in the world. Though famous for the skyscrapers in the city, Hong Kong is also a place full of beautiful nature scenery. This site includes two of my favourite activities to explore the nature, hiking and birdwatching.

Usually, I walk the hiking trail to the Victoria Peak on weekends. It's an easy one and near my home. If with more time, I will walk the Dragon's Back with my friends. But it's quite exhausting and far from my home.

I also like to see the wild birds in the park. Despite its small size, Hong Kong is teeming with bird life. Bird watching provides a path leading to our communication with nature, and enables us to share the enjoyment from the appreciation of birds.
